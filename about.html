About SineVision

Project Objective

Modernizing Film Pre-Production

The traditional pre-production process in the film and TV industry is often lengthy (8-12 weeks) and costly (15-20% of budget). With over 150 major productions annually in Turkey alone, inefficiencies lead to significant resource waste.

SineVision aims to radically improve this by:

  • Slashing script analysis time from weeks to days (Target: 2 weeks to 2 days).
  • Reducing visualization costs by approximately 60%.
  • Minimizing communication-related revisions by up to 80%.

Why Now?

The timing is critical, driven by Netflix's expansion plans in Turkey (3 new studios by 2024), the growing tech needs of local production houses, and the increasing accessibility of AR technology.

Market & Competition

Thriving Turkish Market

Turkey's TV series and film industry is a dynamic sector valued at approximately 1.2 billion TL (2023) with an estimated annual growth rate of 15%.

SineVision's goal is to capture a 5% market share within its first year by offering a tailored, cost-effective solution.

Opportunity

The increasing demand for digital transformation in media and available government incentives (TÜBİTAK, KOSGEB) provide fertile ground for growth.

Competitive Landscape

While established tools exist, SineVision offers distinct advantages:

  • vs. Adobe Creative Suite: Adobe is powerful but costly (approx. 15,000 TL/year) and not specialized for script-to-visualization workflow. SineVision provides a focused solution at significantly lower local pricing (starting 2,999 TL/year).
  • vs. Final Draft: Final Draft is the scriptwriting standard but lacks integrated visualization and collaboration features. SineVision offers a complete pre-production suite.

Our key differentiator is the blend of AI/AR visualization with a collaborative platform, specifically addressing local market needs and pricing sensitivities.
