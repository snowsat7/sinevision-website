Features & Technology

Discover how SineVision leverages AI and AR to revolutionize script visualization and storyboard generation.

Core Capabilities

AI-Powered Script Analysis & Visualization

Upload your script, and our advanced NLP models analyze scene structure, character emotions, dialogue, and action. AI then generates initial visualizations and storyboard drafts, reducing manual analysis time by over 90%.

Intelligent Storyboard Generation

Go beyond simple visualization. SineVision automatically suggests camera angles, shot types, and compositions, creating dynamic storyboards that adapt to your script's nuances.

AR Pre-Visualization On Set

Use our mobile app or AR headsets (like HoloLens) to overlay digital scenes, characters, and props onto the real-world environment. Perfect for blocking scenes and planning shots directly on location.

Collaborative Cloud Platform

A centralized hub for your entire team. Share scripts, visualizations, storyboards, and feedback in real-time. Eliminate confusion from scattered communication channels and reduce revisions by up to 80%.

Psychological & Emotional Insights

Our AI analyzes character arcs and dialogue to provide insights into emotional beats and psychological undertones, helping directors and actors achieve deeper, more resonant performances.

Cost & Time Efficiency

Significantly cut pre-production costs (estimated 60% reduction in visualization expenses) and shorten timelines, allowing resources to be allocated elsewhere.
